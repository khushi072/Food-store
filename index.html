About

Our Restaurant allows you to experience more than just the cuisine. It is the finest representation of Indian culinary traditions through the showcase of the region. The experience of the culture through the food is as subtle as the flavours themselves. In an era when change is the order of the day, our restaurant epitomizes timelessness with a menu that has remained unchanged for almost 4 decades. It stands testimony to the very roots of a culture that is defined by its 5,000-year old tradition.
